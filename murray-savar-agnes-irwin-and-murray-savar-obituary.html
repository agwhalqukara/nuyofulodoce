Murray Savar Agnes Irwin and murray savar obituary

The city of Philadelphia is grieving the sudden loss of esteemed musician and educator Murray Savar, who passed away unexpectedly on Friday, October 27, 2023. Murray was renowned in the local arts community both for his musical talents and dedication to mentoring students. His absence leaves a substantial void.

Early Life and Education

Born and raised in Philadelphia, Murray Savar developed a passion for music from a young age. He studied piano and music theory extensively, demonstrating prodigious talent.

Murray obtained his bachelor's and master's degrees in music locally before embarking on his decades-long performing and teaching career.

Accomplished Musician

As a pianist, Murray Savar used his gifts to serve Philadelphia's religious and cultural institutions for over 30 years. He was on staff at multiple synagogues as a musician and choir director.

Scott Borsky, who collaborated with Murray, described his "sheer love of playing and teaching music" as a joy to behold. Murray shared his musical passion widely.

Dedicated Educator

In addition to performing, Murray was devoted to arts education. He served as the Visual and Performing Arts Department Chair at the prestigious Agnes Irwin School for over 15 years.

Students speak of Murray's ability to inspire excellence and confidence. His balance of high expectations with compassion created an ideal learning environment.

Love of Learning

A lifelong scholar, Murray sought to master diverse musical genres from classical to popular music. His intellectual curiosity was never sated.

Friends recall Murray lighting up when debating musical styles or playing "name that tune." He fully embodied the joys of lifelong learning.


Bringing People Together

With music's power to unite, Murray created connections between people of all ages and backgrounds. His joyous spirit fostered community.

The outpouring of grief since his passing proves just how many lives Murray impacted across Philadelphia. The arts world lost a guiding light.

Keeping His Memory Alive

While processing this painful loss, Murray's loved ones find solace in the rich legacy he leaves behind. They will honor his memory by sharing stories and songs to keep his vibrant spirit alive.

And Murray Savar's students - past, present and future - will carry forward his passion for creativity, learning and community through arts education. Though deeply missed, Murray's indelible impact persists in Philadelphia and beyond.
