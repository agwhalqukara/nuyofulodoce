Whitney Port Dishes on Her Wedding Dress, Location, and More

Here comes the bride, all dressed in… Whitney Eve?

Whitney Port has a lot of decisions to make about her upcoming wedding to Tim Rosenman, but the fashion designer is on the fence about whether she'll wear her own Whitney Eve line on her big day. "I'm not sure yet," she told Us Weekly at the Hunters Alley Launch on March 13 in Los Angeles.

In fact, Port, who got engaged in November in Australia, may turn to her fiance for style inspiration. "I really trust his advice," the fashion designer, wearing her own Whitney Eve summer collection, said. "He has really good taste so I'm always asking for it."

While she hasn't said yes to a wedding dress yet, The Hills alum, 29, has at least narrowed down her vision for her day. "I don't want to be too over the top," she told Us. "Something simple and classic."
