Erin Kelly Bio (Wiki)

Who is Erin Kelly?

Erin Kelly is an American theater, film, and TV series actress. She is best known for her role as Annabelle Tillman in Katherine Brooks’ 2006 film Loving Annabelle.

Erin Kelly: Bio, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Siblings

Erin Kelly was born on August 21, 1981, in San Diego, California, US, to John (dad) and Jan Kelly (mom). She was brought up in her hometown with a younger brother named Jason Curtis Kelly who is a singer now.

When Erin was only nine-year, she moved to Boulder, Colorado along with her family.

Education

She completed her schooling at the age of 16 at the Meisner Technique School. Then she attended Marymount Manhattan College in New York City, where she earned a degree in theater.

Erin Kelly: Professional Life, Career

Television

From 2006 to 2007, Erin Kelly appeared in the Hawaii surfer drama Beyond the Break. That show was filmed on location in Oahu, Hawaii.

Theater

Kelly co-founded the LA Cafe Plays series at the Ruskin Group Theater where different writers, actors, and directors use to perform.

Kelly has appeared in The Vagina Monologues and Picasso at the Lapin Agile with the Ruskin Group Theater. Furthermore, she also appeared in The Miracle Worker (Helen Keller), The Sound of Music (Maria), and Oliver! (Fegan), and Hansel and Gretel (Gretel).

Movies

In 2014, she was in Girltrash: All Night Long as Candace. In 2012 she appeared in Bizarre Love Triangle as Nicole in Hippe Go Yuppie. Likely, she worked for Waking Madison as Grace.

In the year 2009, she was in Sleepy Joe as Jen. Moreover, she was in an Abnormal Abduction like Carol. In 2008, in The Process, she played the girl. And she appeared in Beyond the Break as Erin in 2006.

In the same year, in Loving Annabelle, she played Annabelle Tilman and Kate in the film Finding Kate. In the film Famished, she played Violet.

She has collaborated with director Katherine Brooks on three films.

Producer

Since 2009, Kelly has had her own production company, Rock Rose Entertainment, through which she later launched Hitching Post Theatre and the Indie Colorado Cinema Experience (ICCE), showcasing Colorado films.

She is currently the Artistic Director of the BolderLife Festival, a joint venture between Rock Rose Entertainment and the Bolder Media Group.

Moreover, Kelly was a BolderLife Festival panelist in the year 2013 Special Panel of Women in Television, Film, and Fashion, with Jessica Clark.
