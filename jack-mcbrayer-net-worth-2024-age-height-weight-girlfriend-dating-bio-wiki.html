Early Life

Jack McBrayer was born in the year 1973 on 27th May in Macon in Georgia but soon moved to Conyers when he was just 15 years old. He took admission at the University of Evansville, which is located in Indiana. Over there, he studied theatre management or also known as theatre administration. Other than having an interest in acting in theatre, Jack also was interested in doing comedy. He did sketch comedy and worked at the improvisational comedy enterprise known as The Second City. He also worked at a training center of doing improvisational comedy located in Chicago, which was known as IO Chicago.
