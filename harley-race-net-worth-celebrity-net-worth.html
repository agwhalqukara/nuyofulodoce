What is Harley Race's Net Worth?

Harley Race was an American former professional wrestler and wrestling promoter and trainer who had a net worth of $600 thousand at the time of his death. Harley Race died on August 1, 2019 at the age of 76.

Harley Race was born in Quitman, Missouri in April 1943. As a professional wrestler he made his debut in 1960 and retired in 1991. Race competed in the American Wrestling Association, the National Wrestling Alliance, the World Wrestling Federation, and others. He then worked for World Championship Wrestling as well as the Heart of America Sports Attractions and World League Wrestling. Harley Race was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2004 and won the King of the Ring and the Sam Muchnick Memorial Tournament in 1986. He was an eight time NWA World Heavyweight Champion and was inducted into the NWA Hall of Fame in 2005. Race was also a three time AWA World Tag Team Champion with Larry Hennig. He was also the first ever NWA United States Heavyweight Champion.
